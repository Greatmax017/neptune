ABOUT NEPTUNE SECURITY AUSTRALIA TRADING Ltd(Europe)

Neptune Security Australia Trading Ltd(Europe) is a branch of Neptune Securities Ltd in Europe. We serve customers in European countries with professional, enthusiastic and well-qualified employees. We welcome everyone's supervision and suggestions.

Neptune Securities Ltd ( ABN 65 133 085 908 ) is an Australian leading financial provider involving online financial and trading services. Neptune securities Ltd is authorised and supervised by Australian Securities & Investments Commission (ASIC), and currently holding an Australian Financial Services licence (licence No: 332890 ). Neptune Securities Ltd established in 2009 and located in Sydney, Australia. With more than ten years’ history,  Neptune Securities Ltd focused on providing forex financial service for institutional customers and made outstanding achievements. From 2017, we open our service for retail customers due to expansion of group business. With the movement of financial trading in the last ten years, Neptune Securities Ltd has always been at the forefront of technology and services and provided Mobile Trading and Web trading in succession. What’s more, Neptune Securities Ltd always stick to the position as a STP broker, providing the most open and transparent trading service for each trader with best pricing.
